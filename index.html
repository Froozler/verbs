Paraguayan Voseo Rules 🇵🇾

In Paraguay, standard casual speech uses vos instead of tú. The plural is always ustedes (never vosotros).

Present Tense (Presente)

Drop the -r, add an 's', and put the stress (tilde) on the final vowel. Note: Stem-changing verbs do not change for vos!

TypeTú (General)Vos (Paraguay)
-AR (hablar)tú hablasvos hablás
-ER (comer)tú comesvos comés
-IR (vivir)tú vivesvos vivís
Stem (poder)tú puedesvos podés

Past Tense (Pretérito)

Identical to the standard 'tú' form, but standard Voseo dictates dropping the trailing 's'.

EndingVos FormExample
-AR-astevos hablaste
-ER / -IR-istevos comiste

Imperfect (Imperfecto)

Used for ongoing past actions or "used to". It is identical to the standard 'tú' conjugation.

EndingVos FormExample
-AR-abasvos hablabas
-ER / -IR-íasvos comías
